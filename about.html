My name is Robert and this is my website. I built this site so I could have a visible space to post things I write, images and pictures I design or manipulate, links to other parts of the web that I find entertaining and inspiring, and whatever other types of digital multimedia that I either make or like.

I spend my days working in marketing and advertising. I get to utilize my creative abilites and energies to earn a living, which I really enjoy doing. There are, however, certain parameters that I must work within.

This website serves as an outlet that is unfettered by restrictions or expectations, allowing me to explore and write about some of the more abstract and obscure things that fascinate me, while at the same time acting as a portfolio that showcases work I produce for professional purposes.
